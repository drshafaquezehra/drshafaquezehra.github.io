Communication Skills

Undergraduate Level | Jamia Millia Islamia

Focuses on developing critical thinking, effective academic writing, and comprehensive communication skills through an interactive and multimodal pedagogy.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in fostering a classroom environment that prioritizes interactive learning and critical inquiry. By integrating traditional texts with digital and multimodal media, I aim to equip students with the analytical tools necessary to navigate complex contemporary landscapes. Mentorship and continuous evaluation remain at the core of my teaching practice.
