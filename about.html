Biography

Dr. Shafaque Zehra is an interdisciplinary humanities researcher and educator currently serving as Guest Faculty in the Department of English at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. She holds a Ph.D. in English Literature from Jamia Millia Islamia, where her doctoral dissertation explored the stylistic embedding of marginalization within select graphic novels.

With an academic foundation established at Aligarh Muslim University, where she completed both her B.A. (Honours) and M.A. in English Literature with First Division honors, Dr. Zehra has cultivated a deep expertise in visual storytelling, postcolonial studies, and rhetorical analysis. As a former Senior Research Fellow under the Maulana Azad National Fellowship, her scholarship bridges the gap between traditional literary analysis and contemporary visual and digital cultures.

Currently, her research explores the intersections of AI and literature, higher education pedagogy, and trauma studies, reflecting her commitment to understanding how evolving technologies and narratives shape epistemic justice and cultural memory.
