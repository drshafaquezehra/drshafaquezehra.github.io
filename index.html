About Dr. Shafaque Zehra

Interdisciplinary Humanities Researcher | Guest Faculty, Jamia Millia Islamia

Dr. Zehra is an interdisciplinary humanities researcher specializing in graphic narratives, multimodal discourse analysis, visual rhetoric, and postcolonial visual culture. Her research examines how visual storytelling constructs identity, memory, trauma, marginalization, and epistemic justice across literary and digital environments.

Her recent work extends into AI-assisted reading practices, digital humanities, multimodal literacy, and innovative approaches to higher education pedagogy. She is particularly interested in the evolving relationship between literature, visual communication, technology, and knowledge production.
